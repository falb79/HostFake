What is Fake Reveal

Fake Reveal is a web platform for deepfake video and image detection. We detect video deepfakes based on the inconsistency between lip movement and the audio track, using lip-reading and speech-to-text models.


Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to identify and combat deepfake content effectively.
